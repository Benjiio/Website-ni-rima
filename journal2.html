Managing your digital footprint is more than just staying safe online. It is about protecting your professional reputation and being a responsible team member in a digital world. Since everything we do online, from the emails we send to how we handle company data, leaves a lasting trail, practicing good etiquette and cyber safety is essential for building trust with colleagues and keeping the workplace secure. At the end of the day, being mindful of your digital habits shows that you respect both your own career and the privacy of those you work with.
