This seminar highlights how building a better workplace starts with understanding respect, diversity, and inclusion. Respect is defined as valuing the worth of everyone, whether it’s respecting yourself, others, or even social norms and national symbols. While diversity is about the mix of different backgrounds and perspectives in a team, inclusion is the actual practice of making everyone feel safe and welcome. Essentially, this remind us that respect isn't something you just demand—it’s earned by treating people with empathy and fairness every day.
